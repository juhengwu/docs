Know Your AI helps teams build compliance-ready evaluation workflows with clear evidence trails.
Use evaluations, human review, and monitoring results to document decisions and reduce risk.

## What you can do

- Maintain audit-ready evaluation history
- Track policy and safety issues across releases
- Share evidence with legal, security, and leadership teams

## Typical workflow

1. Run offline evaluations on critical datasets.
2. Use **LLM as the Judge** and **Human Annotate** for review.
3. Export findings to support risk and compliance reviews.

If you need custom compliance reporting, reach out to the Know Your AI team.
